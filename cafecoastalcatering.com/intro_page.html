About

Cheers to 30 years of excellence.

  • December 1983

    Our Humble Beginnings

    It began in the midst of a midwestern winter in 1983. While practically still a teen, Floyd Slyman had opened his first restaurant called "International Sandwich Shop", about the size of your average dorm room. With little money and no experience, Floyd had a pleasant surprise waiting in his future...

  • December 1989

    Bigger and Better

    "International Sandwich Shop" was a success! Floyd then moved on to bigger and better opportunities by opening four more International Sandwich Shops, followed by his first hybrid store, "International Deli & Bakery with Imported Foods".

  • December 1991

    Heading South

    Realizing it was time to move on from his small-town, Slyman moved to Jacksonville, Florida, where he started International Deli. A few stores down, he befriended another store owner, Jay Jabour, who introduced Floyd to his current wife, Karlene. The two got married and opened their first restaurant together, Laura Street Cafe, in the heart of downtown Jacksonville.

  • July 2014

    Corporate Calling

    Following International Deli, Floyd and Karlene went on to open over fifteen restaurants, along with a flourishing catering business. With the growing success, Slyman Restaurants Inc., a privately-owned corporation, was born.

The Faces Behind the Vision

Karlene Slyman

Owner

Known for her bubbly personality and outstanding desserts, Karlene has used her Southern Belle charm to make every customer feel like family. Karlene comes from a background of family-owned luxury clothing stores, established in her hometown of Vicksburg, Mississippi, and has plenty of experience involving working with people. Known for his attention to detail and knowledge of business, Floyd keeps his restaurants running smoothly and efficiently.
